🇿🇦 South Africa Programme

AIR currently provides active academic support for Grade 12 Mathematics and Physical Sciences learners through the AIR Mentorship & Academic Support Network.

Learners from Grades 4 to 11 are also encouraged to register. Their registration helps AIR understand the demand for different grades and subjects while we continue recruiting dedicated volunteer mentors across South Africa.

If the subject you need is not currently available, you will have the opportunity to enter it manually. As AIR expands its network of volunteer mentors, additional grades and subjects will be activated.

🌍 Learners from Other African Countries

AIR currently provides active academic support based on the South African curriculum.

Learners from all African countries are welcome to register. As AIR recruits volunteer mentors who are familiar with your country's education system, registered learners will be contacted when mentoring becomes available.

Please provide your current class or grade and the subjects you would like support with.
