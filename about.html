WHO WE ARE

Empowering Africa Through Collaboration

African Industrial Revolution (AIR) is a non-profit organisation committed to empowering Africa through education, mentorship, research, innovation, technology and collaborative partnerships.

We believe Africa's greatest resource is its people. By connecting learners, mentors, volunteers, educators, researchers, professionals, institutions and organisations, AIR creates opportunities that inspire innovation, strengthen communities and prepare future generations to solve Africa's greatest challenges.

AIR serves as a collaborative platform where knowledge, experience and opportunities are shared across borders, encouraging sustainable development through education, technology and community partnerships.

BUILDING AFRICA THROUGH COLLABORATION

Strong Partnerships Create Lasting Impact

AIR believes meaningful and sustainable change cannot be achieved by one organisation alone. Real progress happens when people, institutions and communities work together toward a shared vision.

We aim to build long-term partnerships with educational institutions, businesses, governments, community organisations, researchers and development partners who share our commitment to empowering Africa through education, innovation and collaboration.

Together we can expand opportunities, strengthen skills development, promote research, inspire innovation and create solutions that benefit present and future generations.

COMMUNITY PROJECTS

Great Communities Are Built Through Great Projects

Across Africa, individuals, schools, universities, organisations and community groups are developing projects that have the potential to improve lives. Many of these projects never move beyond the planning stage because of limited access to collaboration, mentorship, technical support or partnerships.

AIR welcomes community project proposals that aim to create a positive social impact across Africa. We are interested in projects that contribute to education, technology, engineering, agriculture, healthcare, environmental sustainability, entrepreneurship, research, youth development and other initiatives that strengthen African communities.

AIR serves as a coordinating and administrative platform that brings together volunteers, professionals, organisations and communities. Where appropriate, AIR may facilitate collaboration, mentorship or partnerships to help community projects grow and create meaningful impact.

Do You Have a Community Project Proposal?

If you are leading or planning a project that can make a positive difference in your community, we invite you to share a proposal with AIR. We will review your submission and, where possible, explore opportunities for collaboration and support.
