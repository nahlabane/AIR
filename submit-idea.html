AFRICAN INDUSTRIAL REVOLUTION

Community Project Proposal

AIR welcomes proposals for community projects that can improve education, technology, engineering, agriculture, healthcare, environmental sustainability and other initiatives that positively impact African communities.

Important Notice

AIR welcomes proposals for community projects that create positive social impact across Africa.

Please do not submit confidential inventions, trade secrets, patentable concepts, proprietary business information or any commercially sensitive material through this form.

Submission of this proposal does not create a partnership, funding agreement, ownership transfer or any obligation for AIR to implement, finance or adopt your proposal. AIR may contact you if additional information is required.
