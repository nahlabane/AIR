About African Industrial Revolution (AIR)

Building Africa's future through education, mentorship, innovation, research and collaboration.

Who We Are

African Industrial Revolution (AIR) is a non-profit organisation committed to empowering Africa through education, mentorship, research, innovation, technology and collaborative partnerships. AIR connects learners, volunteers, mentors, educators, professionals, entrepreneurs and organisations to create opportunities that strengthen communities across the continent.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading collaborative platform for education, mentorship, innovation, research and community development.

Our Mission

To connect people, institutions, businesses and communities through programmes that promote learning, innovation, skills development and sustainable impact across Africa.

Working Together

AIR believes meaningful change is achieved through collaboration. We aim to partner with schools, universities, companies, government departments, NGOs, community organisations, research institutions and development partners to create opportunities for learners across Africa.

Creating Impact Together

Our impact grows through the combined efforts of learners, volunteers, mentors, educators and partner organisations. Every contribution helps create opportunities for the next generation of African leaders.
